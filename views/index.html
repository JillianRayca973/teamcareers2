In September 2019, a
new club was formed by
three Juniors at Nazareth Academy
High School. It is called T.E.@.M.
Careers Club and it serves as
an outlet for S.T.E.M. information,
innovation, and advancement. Its
main purpose is to spread
awareness of the tech industry,
the gender gap, and
to encourage students to
become excited about these fields.

If you have any
questions about what our
program does, what our
meetings are like, or just
want to explore, please
feel free to check out
the rest of the website
by clicking through the
navigation bar!
